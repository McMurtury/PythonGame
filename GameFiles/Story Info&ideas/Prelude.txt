Life goes on. What's one person's struggle when nothing changes.... Yet....the heroes, the champions of legend refused to give in to the tide of time and the rule of others. They strode forward trusting in their skill and knowledge to pull them from any dangers those above may throw down to them. And while many tried to walk their path, few held true to their heart in it. For one mistep and the end wass quick to follow. Nevertheless, it is time for a new story. A story that shall ring out across the land from village to castle, and that legend and story is yours to make. Choose carefully, for the gods may not take kindly to someone upsetting the order of things.

But....perhaps it is time someone did....
